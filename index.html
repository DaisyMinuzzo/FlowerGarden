Venetian Pizza

Translate

Course Appetizer, merenda

Tempo di preparazione 45 minutes

Cook Time 20 minuti

Tempo totale 1 hour 5 minutes

Ingredients

1 1 busta (7 g) active dry yeast

1 cup (250 ml) di acqua calda

1 1 cucchiaino salt

5 5 cucchiai (75 ml) extra virgin olio oil

Coarsely ground pepe nero

2 tbsp di foglie di rosemary leaves

3 cups (375 to 435 g) di farina

1 1 cipolla grande, chopped

4 roasted tomatoes (8 se sono pomodorini)

16 16 grandi canned flat anchovy fillets

Instructions

  1. Dissolve the lievito in ¼ cup (60 ml) of the water in a large bowl and mettere da parte per provare about 10 minutes. Stir in the remaining water, the salt, three tablespoons (45 ml) of l'olio, il pepe e il rosmarino.
  2. Incorporare circa metà the flour, until the mixture forms a pasta morbida that leaves the sides of the lati della ciotola.
  3. Turn the dough out on una tavola infarinata e impastare about 8 minutes, kneading in farina aggiuntiva until the dough is elastic and no longer sticky.
  4. Inserire la palla di pasta in a clean oiled bowl.
  5. Turn the dough to oil it on all sides, cover, and mettere da parte a lievitare fino al raddoppio, 3 to four hours in refrigerator or 1 hour at temperatura ambiente.
  6. Punch the impasto down.
  7. While the impasto is rising heat the remaining 2 tablespoons (30 ml) olio in una padella. Add the onion and cook very slowly, stirring, for about 20 minutes, until la cipolla è tenera e ha assunto un color oro intenso. Add 2 tablespoons (30ml) cold water to stop the cooking and togliere dal fuoco.
  8. Preriscaldare il forno to 450°F (230°C). Line a baking sheet with pergamena.
  9. Dividere the dough into four portions and forma ciascuno in un giro about 8 inches (18cm) in diameter.
  10. Make the rounds un po 'più sottile around the edges than in il mezzo.
  11. Spread the cipolla on each round.
  12. Coarsely chop the pomodori and scatter them on the cipolla, then top with 4 filetti di acciughe.
  13. Metti su the baking sheet.
  14. Place in the oven on il cestello più basso and bake 15 to 20 minutes, until the dough is leggermente dorato.
